Meat Loaf

Description

This best ever meatloaf recipe lives up to its name and is my favorite I have found.

Try it and you will see.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 slices bread, crumbled
  • Ground beaf
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Carrot, shredded
  • Brown sugar
  • Ketchup
  • Yellow Mustard

Steps

  1. Step 1:Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2: Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and ground black pepper in a large bowl. Add crumbled bread and stir until dissolved. Mix ground beef, onion, Cheddar cheese, and carrot into bread mixture;
    transfer mixture to a 9x5-inch loaf pan. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard in a small bowl; spread over the meat mixture.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 60 to 75 minutes.
    n instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
